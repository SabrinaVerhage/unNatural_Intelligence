How to experience

Open Your Eyes

Open the map and explore. You're looking for (un)naturally intelligent lichens — real organisms you can find growing in quiet places. Check the edge of a stone, the bark of a tree, in between leaves on branches. Lichen are everywhere — once you notice them, you’ll start seeing them all the time.

Find (un)naturally intelligent lichen

Find a lichen and capture it to begin a chat. Each one has their own character and perspective, based on how they grow and where they live. They’re not powered by brains — but by relationships. Talking with them might change how you think about intelligence.

Collect and connect

Every lichen you connect with is added to your collection. Maybe they aren’t just conversations... but links in a larger system, a network of shared intelligence shaped by your choice and attention.

Become (un)naturally intelligent

Intelligence doesn’t require a brain — it can emerge through structure, feedback, and connection. You are part of this distributed intelligence. Intelligence is not isolated. It’s symbiotic.
